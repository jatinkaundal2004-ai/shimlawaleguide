Famous Places in Shimla

About Shimla

Shimla, the capital of Himachal Pradesh, is one of India’s most popular hill stations. Known as the “Queen of Hills,” Shimla is famous for its pleasant climate, colonial architecture, scenic mountain views, and lush green valleys. Once the summer capital of British India, the city still reflects its rich heritage through landmarks like Christ Church, Viceregal Lodge, and Mall Road. Shimla is a perfect destination for nature lovers, adventure seekers, and travelers looking for peace and beauty.
